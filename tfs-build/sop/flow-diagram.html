How to read it. Solid arrows = handoffs inside a system. Dotted arrows = cross system boundaries. Dotted arrows are the break points to watch — that's where things most often fail.

The three break points

  1. Notion → n8n. If Notion isn't showing Blasted=true within ~15 minutes of marking Actively Marketing, the bridge didn't fire. Check n8n.dealpros.io execution log first.
  2. n8n → GHL tag application. If n8n execution log shows success but no SMS went out, the tag was applied but WF02 didn't trigger. This is the WF02 SMS gap — see Troubleshooting in TEAM_SOP.
  3. GHL WF02 → buyer. If WF02 history shows the step fired but the buyer never got it, it's an LC Phone or Mailgun delivery issue. Check the GHL conversation for the contact.
