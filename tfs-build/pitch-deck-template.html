Deal {{DEAL_ID_SHORT}} · Risks
13 · Risks & Key Assumptions

What could move the thesis.

Due-on-Sale (DOS) · Subject‑To

Lender could call the loan on transfer. Historical call rate on seasoned, current SubTo loans with continued payment: < 2%. Mitigation: land trust conveyance, pay reliably, maintain hazard ins in seller‑friendly form.

Insurance Transferability

FL HO policies increasingly name insured‑only. Pre‑close quote obtained from Ovation E&S at $1,728/yr naming buyer LLC as additional insured.

Rent / ADR Assumption Risk

Pro forma uses median comps, not high. 10% downside: LTR to $2,385/mo, STR ADR $193. Model stress-tested at both; cashflow flips to ($720)/mo Yr‑1 under worst case.

STR Ordinance Risk

Tampa requires vacation rental registration. Current rules allow non‑owner‑occupied STR in this zone — verify at close; ordinance review flagged for Q3 '26.

Seller Carry Balloon (Yr 5)

$36K seller 2nd balloons month 60. Plan: amortize cashflow into reserve fund ($600/mo), or refi to retire. Documented in seller‑carry addendum.

Rate Environment · Refi Exits

BRRRR and ADU scenarios assume DSCR refi at 7.25%. +100 bps → BRRRR cash pulled drops by $18.4K. Not a dealbreaker, but compresses the recycle.
