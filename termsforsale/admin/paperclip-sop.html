1. System Overview

Paperclip is the AI operating system that automates Deal Pros' back-office. It scores leads, underwrites deals, matches buyers, sends alerts, and generates daily reports — all automatically.

What's Automated (Don't Touch)
  • Buyer signup → auto-profiled + tagged within 30 min
  • New deal set to "Actively Marketing" → buyers notified within 30 min (SMS + email)
  • Deal alert → 3-day follow-up sprint (Day 0/1/2 SMS + email)
  • CEO briefing → daily 7am AZ to Brooke's phone
  • Weekly synthesis → Monday 8am AZ
  • Seller leads scored within 15 min (Claude AI)
  • Hot leads (35+) auto-underwritten within 30 min
  • Underwriting complete → call prep generated for Eddie
  • Deal package generated on pkg-requested tag
  • Stale lead follow-up after 7 days
  • Partner scorecard every Friday
  • Revenue tracker monthly

3. Deal Status Flow

New Submission → Pending Items → Ready for Review → Accepted → Actively Marketing → Assignment Sent → Assigned with EMD → Closed

  • "Actively Marketing" = deal shows on website + triggers buyer notifications within 30 min
  • "Closed" + Date Funded = shows in CEO briefing + revenue reports
  • Changing status to anything other than "Actively Marketing" removes it from the website

4. VA Responsibilities (Manual Processes)

Daily Tasks
  • Enter all new deal data in Notion with ALL required fields
  • Upload property photos to Google Drive folder, link URL in Notion "Photos" field
  • First photo uploaded = cover photo (sort by upload time)
  • Set deal status to "Actively Marketing" when ready to list
  • Review CEO briefing notes in GHL for action items
  • Monitor GHL for flagged leads (flag-escalate tag)
When a Deal Closes
  • Set Date Funded in Notion (the date funds were received)
  • Set Amount Funded in Notion (total revenue from this deal)
  • Change Deal Status to "Closed"
  • These 3 fields trigger the revenue tracker and CEO briefing automatically
Blog Posts (Deal Spotlights)
  • Use the VA Post Builder at /api/create-post to create deal spotlight blog posts
  • Posts are added to /blog/posts-index.json and appear on the blog page automatically

8. Domain Switch Checklist

Switching from GHL Website to Netlify
  1. In Netlify: Add termsforsale.com as custom domain
  2. In DNS provider: Update A record @ → 75.2.60.5
  3. In DNS provider: Update CNAME www → jolly-marzipan-8fc443.netlify.app
  4. Remove old A/CNAME records pointing to GHL
  5. Wait 5-10 min for propagation
  6. Verify: visit termsforsale.com → should show new site
  7. Netlify auto-provisions SSL certificate
After switch: Both termsforsale.com and deals.termsforsale.com will serve the same Netlify site. All automations, forms, and functions continue working.
