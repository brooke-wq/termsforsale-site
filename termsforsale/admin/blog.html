Create and edit blog posts for termsforsale.com/blog

The blog editor runs on Decap CMS — a git-based content editor. Posts you create here commit directly to the main branch and trigger a Netlify deploy. Two content types are available:

Deal Spotlight Posts
Individual property spotlights — location, numbers, story, buyer fit.
Education & Guides
Creative finance 101, market insights, buyer strategy articles.
Tip: The "Open Full CMS" button above gives you the full-screen Decap editor experience. Authenticating there uses Netlify Identity / git-gateway (separate from this admin password).
