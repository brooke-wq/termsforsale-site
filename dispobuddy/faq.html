Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Dispo Buddy.

The Basics
Dispo Buddy is a JV disposition partner for wholesalers, agents, and bird dogs. If you have a deal under contract and need help finding a buyer, we handle the packaging, marketing, buyer outreach, negotiation, and transaction coordination. You get paid 50/50 at close.
Dispo Buddy is operated by Deal Pros LLC, a real estate company based in Phoenix, Arizona. We run a national buyer marketplace (Terms For Sale) and work with JV partners across the country.
We're a disposition and deal-marketing operation. We don't wholesale directly to sellers — we work with partners who already have deals under contract and help move them to qualified buyers.
Cash assignments, subject-to, seller finance, hybrid/creative, novation, and Morby/stack method deals. If you have a deal structure that doesn't fit neatly into a category, submit it anyway — we'll evaluate.
