What We Look For in a Deal

We review every submission seriously. Here's what makes a deal strong — and what gets deals passed on or delayed.

Honest Criteria · Better Submissions · Faster Reviews
Must-Haves

The Basics: What Every Submission Needs

Before we review pricing, terms, or market — your deal needs to hit these non-negotiables.

Under Contract

The deal must be under contract. No leads, no maybes, no verbal agreements. If it's not locked up, it's not ready.

Direct to Seller

You must be direct to the seller or hold equitable interest through a JV agreement with the direct wholesaler. No daisy chains.

Photos

Interior and exterior property photos are required. We can't market what buyers can't see.

Accurate Numbers

Contract price, asking price, ARV — these must be based on real comps, not hopes.

Access

We need to be able to show the property. Lockbox, appointment, whatever — access is required.

Docs

Purchase agreement, mortgage statements (for creative deals), and any disclosures should be available.

Deal Types

Deal Types We Work With

We handle more than standard assignments. Here's what we accept and what we need for each.

Cash

Cash Assignments

Standard wholesale assignments — you have it under contract, we find a cash buyer.

What we look for

Clean title, realistic spread, property in marketable condition or priced for rehab.

Typical buyer

Fix-and-flip investors, rental buyers, BRRRR investors.

Creative

Subject-To

Buyer takes over existing mortgage. These deals require more info but often have the best margins.

What we need

Loan balance, interest rate, monthly PITI, maturity date, balloon terms, arrears status.

What makes it strong

Low rate, long maturity, no balloon, reasonable equity position.

Creative

Seller Finance

Seller carries the note. Clean terms and reasonable rates make these move fast.

What we need

Loan amount, monthly payment, interest rate, term, balloon, down payment required.

What makes it strong

Low interest, long amortization, reasonable entry, no balloon or long balloon.

Creative

Hybrid / Creative

Deals with both underlying debt and a seller carry component.

What we need

Full picture of both the existing loan AND the seller carry terms.

These are the most complex to evaluate. Give us everything you have.
Specialty

Novation / Morby / Stack

We work with most creative structures. Submit it and we'll evaluate.

Strong Deals

What Gets Deals Moved Fast

Hit these marks and your deal gets prioritized.

Realistic pricing (not inflated ARV or thin margins)
Complete information (all fields filled, docs attached)
Good photos (interior + exterior, well-lit)
Clear access path (lockbox, appointment available)
Reasonable COE timeline (at least 21+ days)
Motivated seller with clear situation
Property in a market with active buyers
Creative deals with strong terms (low rates, long terms)
Common Issues

Common Reasons Deals Don't Move

These are the most frequent reasons deals get passed on or delayed.

Not under contract / verbal agreement only
Daisy chain (you're not direct to seller)
Inflated ARV or unrealistic asking price
No photos or unusable photos
Missing key information (especially on creative deals)
No access to the property
Very short COE (less than 14 days with no extension option)
Property in a market with no buyer demand
Incomplete or unclear deal structure
Partner unresponsive to follow-up questions
