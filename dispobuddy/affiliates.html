FAQ

What counts as a qualified referral?

A qualified referral is a JV Partner who (1) signs up to Dispo Buddy through your affiliate link, and (2) closes at least one deal with us within 6 months. When that first deal closes, you earn the $200 first-deal bonus and then 5% of our net dispo fee on every deal they close for the next 12 months.

When do I get paid?

We pay affiliates once per month via ACH, on or around the 15th, for deals that closed and funded in the prior month. Minimum payout is $100; if your balance is lower, it rolls to the next month.

Is there a limit to how much I can earn?

No. There is no cap on the number of partners you can refer or the number of deals you can earn on.

What if a deal cancels or falls apart?

If a deal cancels, refunds, or never funds, any related commission may be held back or deducted from future payouts. We only pay on closed, funded deals.

Can I also be a JV partner?

Yes. You can be both an affiliate (referring others) and a JV partner (submitting your own deals). They're separate programs and don't conflict.

How do I track my referrals?

Use the Affiliate Dashboard to see clicks, signups, deals, and commissions in real time.
