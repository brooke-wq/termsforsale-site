Affiliate Program Terms

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

The Dispo Buddy Affiliate Program ("Program") is operated by Deal Pros LLC ("Company," "we," "us"). By signing up as an affiliate ("Affiliate," "you"), you agree to these terms and conditions.

2. Eligibility

You must be 18 years or older to participate. The Program is open to individuals and businesses in the United States. We reserve the right to decline or terminate any affiliate account at our sole discretion.

3. Commission Structure

  • $250 flat bonus — paid when a partner you referred submits their first deal through Dispo Buddy.
  • 10% of net assignment fee — paid on every deal closed by your referred partners through Dispo Buddy.
  • $100 volume bonus — paid for every 5 referred deals that close within a rolling 90-day window.

Commission rates may be adjusted with 30 days' written notice. Changes do not apply retroactively to commissions already earned.

4. Tracking & Attribution

  • Referrals are tracked via a unique URL containing your affiliate ID.
  • A 90-day attribution cookie is set when a visitor clicks your link.
  • Last-click attribution model: if a visitor clicks multiple affiliate links, the most recent click receives credit.
  • Self-referrals (referring yourself or your own business entities) are not eligible for commissions.

5. Payment Terms

  • Commissions are calculated at the end of each calendar month.
  • Payouts are issued on the 15th of the following month.
  • Minimum payout threshold: $50. Balances below this carry over to the next month.
  • Available payout methods: Zelle, PayPal, Venmo, check, ACH/direct deposit.
  • You are responsible for any applicable taxes on affiliate income. A W-9 or W-8BEN may be required for payouts exceeding $600/year.

6. Promotional Guidelines

You may promote Dispo Buddy through legitimate marketing channels including:

  • Social media posts and stories
  • Email newsletters (to your own opt-in lists only)
  • Blog posts and content marketing
  • YouTube and podcast mentions
  • Facebook groups and community posts

Prohibited activities:

  • Spam, unsolicited messaging, or bulk SMS/email to purchased lists
  • Misleading claims about earnings, guarantees, or Dispo Buddy's services
  • Bidding on "Dispo Buddy" or related brand terms in paid search
  • Cookie stuffing, click fraud, or any deceptive tracking practices
  • Impersonating Dispo Buddy or Deal Pros LLC

7. Termination

Either party may terminate the affiliate relationship at any time. Upon termination:

  • Earned, unpaid commissions above the minimum threshold will be paid out in the next payment cycle.
  • Pending (unverified) commissions are forfeited.
  • You must remove all Dispo Buddy promotional materials.

8. Limitation of Liability

Dispo Buddy and Deal Pros LLC are not liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from participation in the Program. Our total liability is limited to commissions actually earned and unpaid.

9. Modifications

We may update these terms at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email. Continued participation after notice constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
