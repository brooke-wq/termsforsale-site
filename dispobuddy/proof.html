What separates deals that close

Not every deal moves. Here's the honest breakdown of what works and what doesn't.

Deals that move

  • Realistic pricing with real margin for buyers
  • Complete info and decent photos on day one
  • Responsive partner who picks up the phone
  • Creative deals with terms that actually make sense
  • Markets where we have active buyer demand

Deals that stall

  • Overpriced with no room for the buyer to profit
  • No photos, missing contract details, vague numbers
  • Partner disappears after submitting
  • Complex structure with zero documentation to back it up
  • Rural or hyper-niche market with no buyer base
