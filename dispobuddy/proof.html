What the Process Looks Like in Practice

1

A wholesaler in Phoenix locks up a Subject-To deal — 3-bed, 2-bath in a strong rental market. They have the contract but no buyer pipeline for creative finance deals. They submit through Dispo Buddy.

2

Within 24 hours, our team reviews the deal, verifies the numbers, and confirms it's marketable. We send a JV agreement.

4

Within a week, we have multiple inquiries. An offer comes in. We negotiate terms, coordinate with title, and push to close.

At closing, the assignment fee is split 50/50 through title. The wholesaler gets paid. The buyer gets a good deal. Everyone wins.

What Separates Deals That Close from Deals That Don't

Deals that move

  • Realistic pricing with margin for buyers
  • Complete info and good photos
  • Responsive partner who answers questions quickly
  • Creative deals with strong terms
  • Markets with active buyer demand

Deals that stall

  • Overpriced with thin margins
  • Missing photos or incomplete info
  • Partner goes dark after submission
  • Complex structure with no supporting docs
  • Niche market with no buyer base
