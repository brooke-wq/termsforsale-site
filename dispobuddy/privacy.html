Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Deal Pros LLC, doing business as Dispo Buddy ("we," "us," or "our"), operates the website dispobuddy.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our JV disposition services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Property details and deal information submitted through our forms
  • Contract documents, photos, and supporting materials you upload
  • Partner profile information (entity name, experience level)
  • Messages and inquiries submitted through our platform

We also automatically collect:

  • Browser type, IP address, and device information
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referral sources
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

  • To review, underwrite, and market your submitted deals
  • To match deals with qualified buyers in our network
  • To communicate with you about deal status, offers, and closings
  • To send OTP codes for portal authentication
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send marketing communications (with your consent)

SMS & Email Communications

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages from Deal Pros LLC regarding deal submissions, status updates, closing coordination, and partnership communications. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • GoHighLevel (GHL) — CRM and communication platform
  • Google Analytics — Website analytics
  • Notion — Deal tracking and pipeline management
  • Netlify — Website hosting
  • Google Drive / Dropbox — Document and photo storage (links you provide)

Deal Information

Property details, contract documents, and deal numbers you submit are used solely for the purpose of evaluating, packaging, and marketing the deal to qualified buyers. We do not share your deal information publicly or with third parties outside of potential buyers and title/closing professionals involved in the transaction.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies and browser storage (sessionStorage, localStorage) to maintain your login session, save form progress, and remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features (including portal login) may not work properly.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including OTP-based authentication for portal access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at info@dispobuddy.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a revised date.
